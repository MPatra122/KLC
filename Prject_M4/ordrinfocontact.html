How to Place an Order

We strive to make the ordering process as seamless as possible. Follow these steps to place an order with us:

  • Directly call on the Contact No provided in the Ordering & Contact us giving detailed order requirement and phonepay payment.
  • Visit our Product Catalog page to browse our range of plants and services.
  • Select the items you wish to order and add them to your cart.
  • Once you have completed your selection, proceed to checkout and fill in your contact and payment details.
  • Review your order and submit it. You will receive a confirmation email shortly after.
  • For bulk orders or special requests, please contact our customer service team at orders@greenplants.com.
